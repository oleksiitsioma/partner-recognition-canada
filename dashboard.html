Employee Partner

As the Key Contact, you are invited to frequently visit www.partnerrecognition.com to take full advantage of Ford's Partner Program for your Employees You will be the primary contact to ensure all offers and benefits are communicated to those who are eligible at your company.

Program Overview:

The Ford Motor Company Partner Recognition Vehicle Purchase Program provides exclusive pricing on eligible Ford and Lincoln vehicles.

Who's Eligible

  • X-Plan Supplier: Active employees, retirees and their spouses (no other family members are eligible) at Canadian locations of selected Ford Motor Company and Ford of Canada suppliers are eligible for the X-Plan pricing.
  • X-Plan Fleet Account: Available to all employees of Ford of Canada's selected fleet accounts that continue to use Ford vehicles as their vehicles of choice for fleet needs

Number of PINs

  • Each employee, member or retiree is limited to 2 Personal Identification Numbers (PINs) per calendar year.

Proof of Eligibility for X-Plan Partner Recognition Program:

  • Documentation providing proof of employment from the eligible Supplier Company or Fleet customer in the form of a paystub or official company identification as defined in the individual program rules. For retired employees from an eligible Partner Supplier, proof of retirement will be required.
  • For spouses (X-Plan Supplier Company) a copy of the customer driver's license will be required (as verification of the same household as sponsor), plus proof of sponsor employment (as noted above).
